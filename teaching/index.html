Undergraduate research

I am the current director of the Lab of Geometry at Michigan - LoG(M). Please direct any questions related to LoG(M) to logm-info [at] umich [dot] edu. LoG(M) is an undergraduate math research program. The lab runs 3-4 projects per semester with emphasis on computation and visualization as media for engaging undergraduates in modern mathematical research. Each team of 3 undergraduates works on a semester-long project led by 1-2 faculty advisors who are experts in the mathematical topic.
