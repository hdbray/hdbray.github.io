CV (updated 10/30/2020)

I am a term assistant professor and Mason Experimental Geometry Lab (MEGL) outreach director at George Mason University as of August 2020. Formerly, I was a postdoctoral assistant professor in the mathematics department at the University of Michigan. My postdoc mentor was Dick Canary.
I completed my Ph.D. at Tufts University with Boris Hasselblatt.

Mission statement

I am a mathematician and an educator. I aspire for humanity's collective mathematical toolkit to blossom, and for the people who build this toolkit to thrive. My mission is to craft a mathematical community in which any mathematical enthusiasts can thrive. In particular, I believe the perspectives, the creative contributions, and the humanity of marginalized people, especially people of color and gender variant people, are essential for progress towards these goals. It is part of my mission to build mathematical spaces and communities where marginalized members of our communities thrive.
