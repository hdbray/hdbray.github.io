Mentoring

Letters of recommendation

If you are asking me for a letter of recommendation, please do the following:

  • Include the earliest deadline in your request. If there is no clear deadline, make one up for me.

    Note that I can only guarantee being able to write for you with at least 1 month of notice if this is a first time letter, and 1 week of notice if I have already written for you before. You should ask even if you are late.
  • Explain to me why you have chosen me as a letter writer. Which skills/strengths/training have I witnessed that are relevant to your application? How does my letter complement the other letters in your portfolio?
  • If I agree to write, please remind me regularly of the deadline.
